[mySomeday](/legacy/mySomeday.html) is a basic web application prototype I created at a hackathon while working for RBC. The point was to create an application using IBM Bluemix cloud technology that would benefit RBC as well as improve our customers' experience.

To accomplish this I made a budgeting tool that visualizes customer's account balances, allowing them to adjust their spending and cut down to meet their savings goals in the future.

The idea was well received by judges in the RBC Cloud Computing Challenge as well as a Director within RBC.
