Sushi Shop Tycoon is a Java restaurant simulation game and the first game I ever created back in June 2014. The goal of the game is to make as much money as you can within one year of fun restaurant simulation.

Players can choose to alter Pricing, Advertisement, Staffing and Purchasing factors, all while keeping track of recent news and stats. All of this factors into a calculation that determines customer behaviour and ultimately the amount of sushi you sell.

Please check out the [source code](https://github.com/jeffwang13/JavaGames/tree/master/Sushi%20Shop%20Tycoon) and [screenshots](https://imgur.com/a/NZR1c).
